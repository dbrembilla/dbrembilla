The Washing Magazine

Brief description of the magazine

Welcome to The Blink, the newest magazine dedicated to the exploration of typography and how it can affect the exploration of articles.
This magazine has three issues, aimed at different topics within the contemporary world:

  • Working from home
  • Space Tourism
  • Language Inclusivity

You can open the articles in the dropdown menu from the bottom-left corner if you are using a computer or you can find it in the menu on the top-left corner if you are from mobile. There you can also find the metadata lenses and typographic designs as described below.
You experience the newest typographic innovations by clicking on the different styles, each dedicated to a different era.
If you wish to explore the metadata of this site, you can click on the circle in the aside on the left; there you will find the different metadata options in the checkbox. Remember to check the options after you open the article.
Lastly, if you wish to consult the documentation, contact us or want to copyright strike us, please read the Documentation, the About us section or the Disclaimer above.
We at The Blink wish you a pleasant read!

The purpose of this web site is to explore various types of typographic and layout style for text documents, as an end-of-course project for the "Information Modeling and Web technologies" course of the Master Degree in Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge of the University of Bologna, under prof. Fabio Vitali. The documents contained in this web site have been selected for their length and complexity. Their publication here is not intended to be an alternative or replace their original locations:

  • What we learned about wellness while working from home, and how to use those insights
